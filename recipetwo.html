Bombay potato fishcakes

Here's a recipe for Bombay potato fishcakes:

    Ingredients:
    
    400g potatoes, peeled and diced
    1 tsp ground cumin
    1 tsp ground coriander
    1 tsp turmeric
    1/2 tsp chilli powder
    200g canned salmon, drained and flaked
    1 egg, beaten
    1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
    50g breadcrumbs
    2 tbsp vegetable oil
    Instructions:
    
    Cook the diced potatoes in boiling water for 15-20 minutes, or until tender. Drain and mash with
     a fork or potato masher.
    
    In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed potatoes with the ground cumin, ground coriander, 
    turmeric, and chilli powder.
    
    Add the canned salmon to the mixing bowl and stir to combine with the spiced mashed potatoes.
    
    Add the beaten egg and chopped fresh coriander to the mixing bowl and stir until everything 
    is well combined.
    
    Shape the mixture into 8 equal-sized patties and coat each one in breadcrumbs.
    
    Heat the vegetable oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Fry the patties for
     2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy.
    
    Remove the fishcakes from the pan with a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel-lined
     plate to drain any excess oil.
    
    Serve hot with a side of mango chutney or cucumber raita, if desired.
    
    Enjoy your delicious Bombay potato fishcakes!
